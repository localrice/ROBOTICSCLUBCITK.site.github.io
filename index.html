About us

Do you want to build your own robot? Or an awesome electronics project? Then Robotics Club, CITK is the right place for you. This club is created to bring out the creativity and imagination of students and to give them chance to become familiar with continuously evolving world of robotics. Come forward and join us. Seminars, workshops and tutorials along with equipment, components and workspace eagerly await you!
